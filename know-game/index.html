Know the Game

Rock-Paper-Scissors-Lizard-Spock is a five-gesture expansion of the classic selection method game rock-paper-scissors. It operates on the same basic principle, but includes two additional weapons: the Lizard (formed by the hand as a sock-puppet-like mouth) and Spock (formed by the Star Trek Vulcan salute). This reduces the chances of a round ending in a tie. The game was invented by Sam Kass with Karen Bryla, as "Rock Paper Scissors Spock Lizard".

The game is played and mentioned in several episodes of the TV series "The Big Bang Theory". According to an interview with Kass, the series producers did not originally ask for permission to use the game, but Kass was officially referenced by Sheldon as the creator of the game during the episode "The Rothman Disintegration", after which he states, "Hail Sam Kass!" to which Leonard, Howard, Raj, and Sheldon all then chant "Hail!" while raising their hands.

Each player chooses a hand gesture, revealing them at the same time. The winner is the one who defeats the others. In case of a tie, the process repeats until a winner is found. In "The Big Theory" (almost always), all the boys choose Spock at the same time and they tie repeatedly.
